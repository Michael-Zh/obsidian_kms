## Common Thread

All three sources converge on one insight: **rules beat judgment under emotional pressure.** Nick Maggiulli (reduce to 80%, keep buying), Leopold (infrastructure layer framework), and the momentum trader (formula runs itself) all describe systems that remove the decision-making from the moment of fear or greed. Your project already has this architecture (Boring Investor rule, Sandbox Rule, Bucket separation). The open question is whether the sandbox has a clear enough rule — right now it's "€500 max + active trading allowed" but no entry/exit discipline. That's the one gap worth closing.
