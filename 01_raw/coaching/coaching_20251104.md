**The "Brain Dump" Experiment:**

1. Set an alarm for 1:30 AM called "Brain Dump."
2. Sit down (not in bed) with a physical notebook and a pen.

- The idea for a dance piece.
- The email you need to send.
- The insight about your strategy doc.
- The worry about your timeline.
- The piano chord you want to learn.

5. Get it all out of your head and onto the paper.

The rule is this: **Once it's on the paper, your brain's job is done.** It is _captured_. It no longer needs to _rehearse_ the idea all night to keep it safe. It can trust that the paper will hold it until morning.

This one act directly addresses your feeling of "I need to note this down now." It says: "Yes, you do. Here is the time and place."
