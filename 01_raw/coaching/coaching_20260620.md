**Diagnosis:** Not a motivation problem. Two structural conflicts:
1. Sleep: phone is the only wind-down tool, but has no end condition
2. Fasting: training window (6–8:30pm) creates a pre-class hunger gap → post-class overeating → late/heavy eating → delayed sleep

**Solution: Two-track protocol**

| Track | Trigger | Target | Method |
|---|---|---|---|
| **A** | Home by 10:30pm (Wed Jazz, most weekdays) | 11pm–12am sleep | Shower + heat food on arrival → eat → 30-min phone timer → bed |
| **B** | Home after 10:30pm (Mon Iyengar, Thu sauna) | 1–2am, no pressure | Normal wind down, no target |

Thursday sauna nights are Track B by default — remove from the "failure" mental account entirely.

**Pre-class eating habit:** On training days, eat a proper meal at work before leaving (5–5:30pm): rice/sandwich + protein. Prevents post-class hunger crash and late-night overeating.

**Sunday Meal Prep — 20-min passive protocol** (after gym session):
1. Start rice cooker (4–5 days of rice)
2. Boil 8–10 eggs (grab-and-go protein all week)
3. Batch one protein source (chicken/tofu/tin fish — oven or pan, no monitoring needed)

All three run while showering. First execution: this Sunday (2026-06-22).

**POS connection:** This is the same "rule fires automatically before emotional pressure arrives" pattern as the Daily Tactical SOP and the Satisficing patch. External structure replaces willpower at the moment of decision.
