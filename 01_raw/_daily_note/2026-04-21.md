Thoughts about AI: looking at the current AI trend: a) since majority of the AI outputs are still generated for human, human judgement and human-involved decision making are still very important, especially for things that are at high stake and low allowance of error; b) it's hard to guarantee consistent AI output, so for anything that needs consistency it's a bit tricky; c) even though AI is very good at process large amount of info and reduce pure labor work, people who can understand and clarify the requirement/need, translate those to decisions, have an eye to judge if the output of AI is meeting those needs, and take responsibility of those decisions are taking more and more important roles.
