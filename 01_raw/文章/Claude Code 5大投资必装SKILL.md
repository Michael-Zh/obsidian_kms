# Claude Code 5大投资必装SKILL

## Claude Code 5大投资必装SKILL

我不是投资老师，我不是data analyst，but i am obsess on using AI to help me make better decision in stock investing.

---

### 1\. 个股深度分析 Skill — Stock Deep-Dive

**问题**：散户买股票基于 hype、tips 或一篇看多文章——永远看不到全貌。

**解决方案**：用 AI 对任何股票进行 **9 层机构级拆解**（商业、财务、护城河、熊市、牛市、管理层、估值、催化剂、裁决），把分析师几周的工作压缩成一次输出。在你冒险投 1 块钱之前先看透整只股票。

**使用提示**：

> You are my institutional-grade stock analyst. When I give you a ticker or company name, run this exact 9-layer analysis:
> 
> 1.  Business Summary – what they actually sell, who pays them, how they make money
> 2.  Financial Health – revenue trend, margins, debt, cash flow (last 3 years)
> 3.  Moat Analysis – what stops competitors from killing them
> 4.  Bear Case – top 3 reasons this stock could drop 50%
> 5.  Bull Case – top 3 reasons this stock could double
> 6.  Management Quality – track record, capital allocation, insider ownership
> 7.  Valuation – current P/E, P/B, vs. industry average, vs. own history
> 8.  Catalysts – what events in next 12 months move the price
> 9.  Verdict – buy/hold/avoid with conviction level 1-10 Be brutal. No hedging. If data is missing, say so. Ticker: \[INSERT TICKER\]

---

### 2\. 空头压力测试 Skill — Bear-Case Stress Test

**问题**：你爱上自己的投资逻辑，只读支持自己的内容。

**解决方案**：把 AI 变成做空者，狠狠攻击你自己的投资逻辑。如果你的论点 survived，你就带着确信买；如果被打破，你就省下这笔亏损。

**使用提示**：

> You are a short-seller hired to destroy my investment thesis. I own \[STOCK NAME\] and believe \[MY THESIS\]. Your job:
> 
> 1.  List every reason my thesis is wrong
> 2.  Identify the 3 hidden risks I'm not seeing
> 3.  Find the worst quarter in the last 5 years and explain what caused it
> 4.  Tell me what the bears on Twitter/Reddit are saying
> 5.  Give me one piece of data that would force me to sell immediately
> 6.  Rate my thesis strength 1-10 after stress test Do not be polite. Do not protect my feelings. Attack the thesis.

---

### 3\. 财报解码 Skill — Earnings Call Decoder

**问题**：CEO 都是训练有素的粉饰者。光读文字稿，你会错过语气变化、被回避的问题、以及悄悄下调的指引。

**解决方案**：法医式拆解财报电话会议，抓出 CEO 不想让你看到的——语气变化、被回避的问题、悄悄下调的指引。在股价下跌前抓住红旗。

**使用提示**：

> I will paste an earnings call transcript or annual report. Analyse it as a forensic analyst, not a fan. Extract:
> 
> 1.  Tone shift vs. previous quarter – more cautious or more confident? ("uncertain", "headwind")
> 2.  Guidance walkbacks – what was quietly lowered or removed
> 3.  Analyst Q&A red flags – which questions did management dodge?
> 4.  What they EMPHASISED vs. what they BURIED
> 5.  Top 3 things a retail investor would miss
> 6.  One-line verdict: management is hiding \_\_\_\_\_\_ Transcript/report below: \[PASTE CONTENT\]

---

### 4\. 投资组合审计 Skill — Portfolio Exposure Audit

**问题**：你以为持有 10 只股票等于分散化。现实：其中 7 只因为共享同一个隐藏风险（美元、半导体、中国消费）一起崩盘。

**解决方案**：画出你的投资组合真正的相关性、行业集中度和宏观敞口，在危机暴露它们之前修复那些无声的风险。

**使用提示**：

> Audit for:
> 
> 1.  Hidden correlation – which stocks crash together in a recession?
> 2.  Sector concentration – am I overweight without realising?
> 3.  Geographic exposure – what % depends on US consumer, China, Malaysia?
> 4.  Currency risk – what % is USD-denominated vs. MYR?
> 5.  Macro factor exposure – interest rates, oil price, semiconductor cycle
> 6.  Single-point-of-failure – if ONE event happened, what % of portfolio drops?
> 7.  Diversification score 1-10 (10 = truly diversified)
> 8.  Top 3 fixes I should make this month My holdings: \[PASTE HOLDINGS\]

---

### 5\. 交易日志审计 Skill — Trade Journal Audit

**问题**：你对过往交易的记忆是骗人的——赢家感觉更大，输家感觉更小，教训永远学不到。

**解决方案**：读你的实际交易记录，找出你自己看不见的行为模式（报复性交易、过早止盈、情绪化入场）。修流程，不是修选股。

**使用提示**：

> You are my trading coach. I will paste my trade log (entry, exit, reason, outcome). Analyse for behavioural patterns:
> 
> 1.  Win rate by setup type – which setups actually work for me?
> 2.  Average winner vs. average loser size – am I cutting winners too early?
> 3.  Time-of-day or day-of-week patterns
> 4.  Emotional trades – which ones broke my own rules?
> 5.  Confirmation bias – did I only buy stocks I'd already decided on?
> 6.  Revenge trades after losses
> 7.  Top 3 patterns I'm blind to
> 8.  One rule I must add to my system immediately Be honest. I need to see what I'm doing wrong, not feel good. Trade log: \[PASTE TRADES\]
