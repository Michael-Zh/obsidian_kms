# [[Information-Overload]]

## Problem

- Information explosion: if I only receive without processing/transforming, it becomes anxiety (ref: [[2026-03-29]])
- Without understanding personal relevance/actionability, can't let go safely—becomes either anxiety or useful action (ref: [[2026-03-29]])

## Note-Taking Friction & Information Capture

- **Lost Knowledge**: Forgot and lost track of books, authors, movies, YT videos due to no systematic capture (ref: [[2026-05-06]])
- **Context Gap**: Even when remembered, no time to read/watch immediately; don't know why it's relevant without context (ref: [[2026-05-06]])
- **Ideal Process**: Get summary + highlights (esp. sections relevant to current interests), then decide when/if to consume full content (ref: [[2026-05-06]])
- **Value Intent**: Want knowledge as actionable steps to experiment with or improve life (new workouts, note-taking methods, habits) — not just information (ref: [[2026-05-06]])
- **Recommendation Gap**: Want suggestions based on current interests and priorities, not generic lists (ref: [[2026-05-06]])
- **Tool Friction**: Rarely use voice recording; rely on Apple Notes most of the time (ref: [[2026-05-06]])
- **Link Capture Friction**: Annoying to copy video/social post links and paste them to retain context (ref: [[2026-05-06]])
- **Thought Linking Problem**: Don't know how new thoughts relate to existing ones — are they new or building on previous ideas? What can I do with them? (ref: [[2026-05-06]])
- **Review Gap**: Once documented, no way to review thoughts based on current priority (ref: [[2026-05-06]])
- **Discussion Retention**: Even in canvas format with AI, hard to retain key info from discussions (ref: [[2026-05-06]])

## Solution Direction

- Build note sorting and thought capture agent—this is main motivation for KMS project (ref: [[2026-03-29]])
- Need personal knowledge system to make information actionable and meaningful
- Need to integrate captured items into priority-aware workflow
- Create pipeline: capture → summarize → contextualize → recommend → actionable steps
