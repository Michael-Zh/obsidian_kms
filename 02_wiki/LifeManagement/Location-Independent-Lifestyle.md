# [[Location-Independent-Lifestyle]]

## The Critical Priority

Location-independence emerged as the #1 lifestyle goal — more foundational than any single career move. (ref: [[coaching_insight_20251104]])

**Why it matters**: Not just flexibility for you, but ability to be with parents if they need you, to maintain connection with China, to live where you want in Europe.

**The Filter**: This single desire changes EVERYTHING about career strategy.

## The Core Question

**Is your current data analyst role already location-independent, or can it become so?**

This one answer determines the entire next phase:

### IF Role IS or CAN BECOME Flexible
✓ **Golden Ticket**: This is your financial engine for portfolio career
✓ Don't need to "replace" it
✓ Leverage stability to fund movement work
✓ Focus on certifications (Iyengar, Gyrokinesis), not Master's degree
✓ Stay on [[Portfolio-Career-Design]] path

### IF Role is NOT Flexible
⚠ **Real Priority**: Find new location-independent data/strategy role
⚠ Not Dance Therapy Master's (end game, not path)
⚠ Not corporate role replacement
⚠ Could be freelance consulting, remote roles, portfolio businesses

## What Location-Independence Enables

1. **Family Connection**: Flexible to go to China when parents need you
2. **Geographic Choice**: Work from anywhere in Europe (or globally)
3. **Lifestyle Design**: Morning routines, training schedules, teaching times not locked to one office
4. **Portfolio Flexibility**: Can prioritize teaching/movement work weeks, then corporate work weeks
5. **Relationship Stability**: Physical space with partner (separate rooms, travel) as needed

## Current Status Assessment Needed

- Is current OTA data role remote-capable?
- Are there hybrid/flexible options within current company?
- What would need to change to make it location-independent?
- Timeline for making change (if needed)?

## Potential Paths to Location-Independence

### Path 1: Make Current Role Remote
- Negotiate with current employer
- Corporate roles increasingly flexible post-pandemic
- Fastest path if available

### Path 2: Find New Remote Data Role
- Remote-first companies (tech, SaaS, fintech)
- Leverage OTA + strategic thinking background
- May accept salary trade-off for flexibility

### Path 3: Consulting/Freelance
- Build independent consulting practice
- Use existing expertise (OTA, data, strategy)
- Higher autonomy, variable income stability
- Could support movement teaching portfolio

### Path 4: Location-Independent Hybrid
- Part-time remote role (60-70%)
- Part-time teaching/movement (20-30%)
- Portfolio career structure explicitly built in

## Integration with Portfolio Career

Location-independence + Portfolio Career is the winning combination:

| Component | How It Works |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Financial Engine** | Remote/flexible data role (60-70%) pays bills from anywhere |
| **Creative Pillar** | Teaching + practice (20-30%) built into weekly rhythm |
| **Family/Geographic** | Can be in Netherlands, visit China, work from anywhere |
| **Movement Practice** | Uninterrupted 5 AM time for projects; no office commute constraints |

This structure honors all your needs: stability, creativity, family, flexibility.

## Decision Timeline

**Before** committing to Master's degree or major life changes:
1. Clarify: Is current role flexibly located already?
2. If not: What's the timeline to find/make it flexible?
3. Then: Structure portfolio around that foundation

**The Real Goal**: Not to replace your job. It's to make your job _serve_ your portfolio instead of _dominating_ it.
