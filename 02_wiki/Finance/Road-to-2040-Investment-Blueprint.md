## Keep Buying Under Pressure — Validation and Limits (ref: [[在當下瘋狂的股市裡，你應該持續買入嗎？對話『Just Keep Buying』作者 Nick Maggiulli|Nick Maggiulli YT 2026]])

(new) **Even the author broke his own rule**: Nick Maggiulli reduced from 100% equities to 80/20 in 2025 when AI market felt irrational (Nvidia P/S ratio matching Microsoft's 1999 peak). He then turned bullish again in 2026. Lesson: behavioral drift happens to everyone. The system exists for exactly this reason — to fire before the emotional pressure arrives.

(new) **The 5-year time horizon is the primary filter**: Money needed within 5 years → do NOT buy equities right now, regardless of strategy. Money for 20–30 year horizon → yes, keep buying. This directly confirms Bucket 1 (Q3 2029, ~3 years) = 0% equity is structurally correct.

(new) **AI market thesis**: Both bubble AND paradigm shift simultaneously — "that's why it's so confusing." If the answer were obvious, there'd be no debate. Framework: over-investment is guaranteed in any paradigm shift; the question is just how much and whether it's already happened.

(new) **Satisficing principle reinforced by Maggiulli**: He now aims for "good enough" — hitting acceptable return targets, not chasing maximum alpha. Directly mirrors the existing satisficing rule in this blueprint. The math of consistent buying beats perfect timing even if you could achieve perfect timing.

(new) **Valuation compression ≠ business deterioration** (ref: [[MSFT、META、NFLX 的加仓窗口打开了，长线投资者的机会｜基本面，三情景目标价，买点分析|MSFT META NFLX 分析]]): MSFT -30%, META -28%, NFLX -40% from 52-week highs in 2025 — all driven by rate-driven P/E multiple compression, NOT earnings downgrade (all three had *upward* earnings revisions during the same period). For long-term investors, these two scenarios have opposite implications: business deterioration = reduce; multiple compression = opportunity.

(new) **Three-scenario target price framework** (from the MSFT/META/NFLX analysis): Bull/Base/Bear scenarios with specific falsifiable signals. Useful mental model for Tier 3 sandbox entries. Before entering a position: define the specific signal that would invalidate the thesis — not just "price drops."
