# [[Work-Performance-Anxiety]]

## Insights

- **Manager Relationship**: Feel very insecure when meeting with manager J. Unsure if working hard enough during hours, or if lazy, or being manipulated without awareness (ref: [[2026-03-13]])
- **Impact Desire**: Want to feel useful and impactful; aspire to be the expert in area and sought for knowledge (ref: [[2026-03-13]])
- **Decision-Making**: Want to learn to make decisions quickly, use trial-and-error to learn from mistakes (ref: [[2026-03-13]])
- **Task Prioritization**: Want to identify main issues quickly and prioritize tasks effectively (ref: [[2026-03-13]])
- **Moving Target**: Project keeps changing scope and resources. Hard to grip on how to perform and prove myself—feeling like chasing a moving target (ref: [[2026-04-12]])
- (new) **Imposter Spiral Pattern**: When facing an ambiguous analytical problem (upsell opportunity detection), a clear spiral emerges: (1) feel insecure about a method I don't fully understand; (2) hate that it's already been announced to a wide audience; (3) feel stupid for not being able to navigate the vagueness; (4) compare unfavourably to J's business sense and wonder if I'll ever match it; (5) enter fix-it-now urgency loop — head spinning, can't relax, feel "sleepy" when trying to work on it (ref: [[2026-05-31]])
- (new) **The urgency trap**: The anxious response to feeling incompetent is to want to fix it immediately — which ironically makes it *harder* to think clearly. Sitting with the ambiguity rather than forcing resolution is the skill being tested (ref: [[2026-05-31]])
- (new) **Moving target, again**: The upsell project surface changed again this week — J flagged the airline is too small, then on 2026-06-04 gave a new specific analytical direction (compare airline's fare mix by haul type vs. industry). Feeling of chasing a moving target is recurring — not unique to one project, but a pattern in how J works (ref: [[2026-06-04]])
- (new) **Signal pass-through anxiety**: As POC between J and FBU's Vivi, feeling exposed: fear of (a) passing the wrong message and creating misunderstanding, (b) not asking the right questions or influencing Vivi correctly, (c) not even knowing where the project is heading — can't steer what I don't understand (ref: [[2026-06-04]])
- (new) **Methodological discomfort**: Uncomfortable presenting a high-level fare-mix comparison as an "opportunity" without controlling for price offered, fare family design, routes, and available flights. Legitimate analytical concern — but also a perfectionism trigger. The question: when is methodological caution valid vs. a delay tactic driven by anxiety? (ref: [[2026-06-02]])
