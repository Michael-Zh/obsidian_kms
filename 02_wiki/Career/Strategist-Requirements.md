## Insights

- Human judgment and decision-making are critical, especially for high-stakes, low-error-tolerance decisions
- Ability to understand and clarify requirements, translate to decisions, judge if AI output meets needs, and take responsibility are increasingly important
- Strategist role is to diagnose root problems, create strategic direction, align matrix stakeholders, translate strategy into roadmap with KPIs, and ensure execution momentum
- Strategist becomes the "driver of movement" and "voice of direction" — turning vague problems into structured, actionable direction
- Strategy work requires: fact base, stakeholder alignment, ownership clarity, cross-functional glue, execution tracking

## Agent/Product Logic Framework (ref: [[不愧是字节挖来的PM，这Agent逻辑讲得太透]])

**(new) Four-layer funnel for evaluating new AI features** — applicable to product decisions at work AND to own LMS/project development:

1. **伪需求过滤 (Filter fake needs)** — Apply first-principles pressure: "What is the actual friction point? What % is caused by rigid rules or slow feedback loops?" Only that category is what an Agent can actually solve.
2. **True problem quantification** — No "feels weak." Must become: "day-2 retention -X%, Y% exit at step 3." PM must pull logs, do path attribution, translate pain into a measurable gap.
3. **Solution trade-off: fine-tune vs. workflow vs. prompt chain** — Decision matrix: effect-priority + sufficient data → fine-tune; clear logic + high variability → workflow; fast iteration, low cost → prompt chain + fallback.
4. **Success preview & risk contingency** — Before launch: define success metric, failure signal (when to pause), and degradation plan (how to roll back cleanly).

**Key quote:** "Biggest fear isn't too many requirements, it's 'bloated' requirements — big in size, full of water. Our job is liposuction: extract the hardest, most load-bearing core problem that the Agent can actually solve."

**Application to LMS:** When scoping new modules (e.g., meal prep assistant, Microaggression database), run this four-layer filter first. Prevents building the wrong thing.
