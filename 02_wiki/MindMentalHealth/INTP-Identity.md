# [[INTP-Identity]]

## Insights

- **MBTI Type**: I'm INTP but feel my behavior differs from type. I'm a thinker but too many thoughts without execution cause discomfort (ref: [[2026-03-29]])
- **J-Style Management**: Using J-style management to structure thoughts allows me to enjoy the thinking process and get results (ref: [[2026-03-29]])
- **Logical vs. Creative**: Unsure if logic/order is J-exclusive. My P shows in: many ideas, strong curiosity, unwillingness to be constrained by existing rules, BUT also need rules to provide foothold and starting point (ref: [[2026-03-29]])
- **Risk Preference**: Not particularly adventure-seeking; prefer special experiences within safe premises (ref: [[2026-03-29]])

- **Pattern-Finding Obsession**: Obsessed with finding underlying patterns, rules, and logic to simplify thinking (rationale over memorizing). Uses this to understand preferences, aesthetic tastes, attraction patterns — what do they say about me? (ref: [[2026-05-14]])
- **Conscious Decision-Making**: Likes knowing what he likes and doesn't like so choices become deliberate and self-aware, not reactive (ref: [[2026-05-14]])
- **Connection Seeking**: Finds connections between disparate things (common features among men I find attractive, art I find interesting) as a way of self-knowledge (ref: [[2026-05-14]])
