## Staying in the Netherlands — Three Conditions (ref: [[🇳🇱长期愿意留在荷兰的人都有这三个共同点]])

**(new) The three common traits of people who willingly stay long-term in NL:**
1. Satisfied with their work or career here (at minimum: not actively disliking it; ideally: sense of progress and self-actualization)
2. Deep, stable emotional connections in NL (friendships, partnership, community)
3. Locally-rooted daily hobbies with high place-specificity

**Annotation resonance:** All three conditions are currently being met to varying degrees — career satisfaction is conditional (Priority 2), Jeroen relationship is the primary connection (Priority 3), and dance/movement community is the local-rooted hobby. This acts as a self-diagnostic against the "should I stay" question: the weakest leg is the career satisfaction, specifically the "meaning" gap, not the income.

**Connection to 围城 (world as siege) framing from Egypt article:** staying in NL isn't the paradise option — it's the set of problems you've chosen. The question is whether those problems are liveable and growable. For now: yes.
