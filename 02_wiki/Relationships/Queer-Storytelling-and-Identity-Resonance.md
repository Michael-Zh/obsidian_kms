## Emotional Connection to Stories

Watched "Heated Rivalry" (BL hockey romance) and found deep resonance:
- "I've cried several times watching this"
- Powerful emotional pull: the struggle, hiding, fear, courage to come out to family and world
- Recognition: "Finding someone you like gives you courage and safety" (ref: [[2025-12-27]])

**Key Theme**: Queer media serves as both mirror (seeing yourself) and model (seeing possibility)

## Emotional Sensitivity & Expression

Michael is deeply emotional:
- Cries at queer movies (people finally being together, being authentic)
- Deeply moved by themes of sacrifice, love, family separation
- Tears at stories showing: hidden identity → courage → freedom
- Also emotional about: parents aging without children nearby, grandparents' love, rescue/survival narratives, interdimensional father-daughter connection (Interstellar)

**Reframe**: This emotional depth is not weakness. It's somatic intelligence—ability to feel stories in body and translate wordless experience into meaning. (ref: [[00_system/_POS]])
