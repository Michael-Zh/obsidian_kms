## Timeline & Intention

**Original Timeline** (from 2017-02-06): Coming out to parents around 32-35 years old

**Current Status**: Growing toward authenticity and courage to be fully known

**Core Belief** (from "Shame" book reading): Don't carry burden alone. Share problems with family rather than shouldering everything privately. People's lives are their own to live. (ref: [[2017-02-06]])

- **Family Gatherings**: Witnessing family milestones (marriages, children) while privately navigating own path
- **Feeling Isolated**: "Everyone in my generation married with kids; I'm the only single one"
- **Acceptance Shift**: Moving from "maybe I'll have family" to "my life is my own to lead"

**Confidence Building Goal**: Have confidence in managing own life around age 30, then prepare for family conversation (ref: [[2017-02-06]])

**Shame Work**: Reading and understanding that hiding doesn't protect family; authenticity + support is healthier (ref: [[2017-02-06]])

## Emotional Processing — Dreams & Distance

- (new) **Father hospital dream (2026-05-23)**: Three years after coming out, this recurring dream pattern still surfaces. Father unwell, high-risk test, emotional weight of wondering if his health is linked to processing the coming-out. Reflection: "With my parents, things are sometimes better, sometimes worse. I haven't really shared my actual life with them — my relationship, my dance investment." The desire isn't just to share; it's to receive their recognition, blessing, and support (ref: [[2026-05-23]])
- (new) **Pre-coming-out parallel**: This dream echoes the mother-in-hospital dream from years before coming out. The pattern is the same — parental health as externalised guilt/anxiety about authenticity gap (ref: [[2026-05-23]])
- (new) **Elderly care distance**: Jeroen can return to care for his mother easily. Distance to China means only phone calls across time zones. "If there's a serious illness, I'll be too far. Parents would have to manage on their own." This is a real, unresolved structural problem — separate from the authenticity question but emotionally entangled with it (ref: [[2026-05-23]])
