# Keren Rosenberg

## Who She Is

Amsterdam-based choreographer, somatic facilitator, and creator of the Body-Voice Method. Her work sits between contemporary dance, physical theatre, and somatic experience design. She draws from Gaga movement language (Ohad Naharin's system) and integrates trance, voice, and emotional release into structured group containers.

## Core Philosophy

- **Package the wild.** Her central move is taking raw, intense, potentially chaotic sensory experiences (club culture trance states, Gaga's improvisational intensity, emotional release) and giving them a safe structural container. The container makes the experience accessible to non-dancers; the wildness makes it transformative.
- **Choreography as curation.** Works like *Going Primal* and *Not a Love Story* explore primal states and social conditioning — she's not just making dances, she's designing experiences that allow something to happen in the audience/participants.
- **Brand language as philosophy.** Her website copy is poetic and value-laden — a deliberate contrast to the clinical positioning of studios like Anna Mora. Different positioning path, same somatic space.
