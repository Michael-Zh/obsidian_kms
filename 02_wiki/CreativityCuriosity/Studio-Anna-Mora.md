# Studio Anna Mora (Anna Mora)

## Who She Is

Amsterdam-based movement practitioner and studio founder. Integrated Gyrotonic (Gyrokinesis), Pilates, and physiotherapy rehabilitation into a single studio positioning: *"Where movement meets therapy."* Hosts Gyrotonic Teacher Training programs — operating as a platform provider, not just a practitioner.

## What the Studio Models

**The evolution from practitioner → platform curator.** The business architecture has three tiers:

1. **Direct practice:** Personal teaching (Gyrotonic, Pilates private sessions)
2. **Curated multi-practitioner space:** Studio houses other teachers — shifts from selling her own hours to curating a service environment
3. **B2B teacher training:** Hosting Gyrotonic certification programs — the highest-leverage model, where the output is more practitioners, not more clients

This is the progression from active to passive income in a somatic context.

## Business Architecture (as a Strategy Document)

Read the studio website as a business strategy case study:
- **Menu structure:** How the service categories are organized (rehab → Gyrotonic → Pilates) signals the target customer journey
- **Introductory packages:** How new clients are onboarded and price-tiered
- **Teacher training positioning:** How she became the Amsterdam hub node for Gyrotonic certification
