# Celeste Pereira

## Who She Is

Physiotherapist and movement educator. Translates complex biomechanics, neuroscience, and pain science into formats that movement teachers without medical backgrounds can actually use. Her brand sits at the intersection of evidence-based rehab and physical practice education.

## Core Approach

- **Engineer's logic applied to the body.** The book title says it directly: *The Yoga Engineer's Manual*. She disassembles postures and movement patterns the way an engineer disassembles a machine — identifying load paths, failure modes, structural requirements.
- **Myth-busting as entry point.** Uses evidence-based science to challenge received wisdom in yoga/dance (e.g., why common stretching cues create neural pain rather than releasing it). Controversy-as-hook that funnels into deeper content.
- **Progressive fidelity content architecture.** Short Instagram video (precise, single-concept) → podcast depth → book/course (full system). Each layer converts the previous audience upward.
