**Personal Museum App** (ref: [[2026-05-11]])
- Inspired by ArtScanAI; personal museum archive for tracking visits, artworks, labels
- **Pain points to solve**: No auto-location from image metadata; can't upload multiple images per artwork; no social function; can't batch import images; doesn't load existing museum collection info
- **Business model idea**: 3-5 free visits/museum, then charge; trial model
- **Competitor landscape** (Perplexity research):
  - *Artoto* — closest fit: snap artwork, capture label, build private collection archive; very new (5.0/5, 3 ratings)
  - *Smartify* — mature "Shazam for art"; best for audio tours during visits (4.6/5, 7.6K reviews)
  - *Google Arts & Culture* — massive database, poor visit-based personal archive (10M+ downloads)
  - *Artwork Archive* — best for structured cataloging/collectors, not casual museum visits (5.0/5, 92 reviews)
  - *Magnus* — location-based art discovery; niche, limited regional coverage
- **Ideal stack**: Artoto/Smartify for in-visit capture → export to personal archive system
