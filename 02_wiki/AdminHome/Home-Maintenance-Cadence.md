# Home Maintenance Cadence

(new) **Core principle** (ref: [[照着这张表，把家里慢慢理顺|照着这张表]]): Chore overwhelm comes from mixing cadences, not from total volume. Separating daily habits (automatic) from weekly tasks (scheduled) from monthly projects (planned) reduces cognitive load. Don't revisit a once-a-month task every day.

## Daily (automatic habit layer)
- Dishes / dishwasher
- Wipe kitchen surfaces after cooking
- 10-minute evening tidy (everything back to its place)
- Bathroom quick wipe after shower

## Weekly (scheduled block)
- Vacuum / sweep / mop
- Bathroom deep clean
- Laundry (wash + fold + put away same session)
- Take out trash
- Restock basics (check fridge, replenish household supplies)

## Monthly (planned session)
- Deep clean appliances (oven, microwave, kettle)
- Window glass and ledges
- Fridge interior clean-out
- Washing machine self-clean cycle

## Quarterly (project)
- Reorganize drawers and storage
- Descale and deep-clean bathroom fixtures
- Replace: filters, toothbrush heads, household consumables audit
- Clothes review: what to donate/discard
