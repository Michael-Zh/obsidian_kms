# [[Plant-Care-System]]

## Collection Overview

Three biological groups requiring different care protocols:

**Group A — Drought-Tolerant / Epiphytic**
- Sansevieria Laurentii (root rot surgery needed), Sansevieria Kirkii (dehydrated), Succulent/Jade (severe etiolation), Platycerium Staghorn Fern (soil suffocation)

**Group B — Heavy Drinkers / Ficus & Canes**
- Ficus elastica Tineke (lower leaf necrosis), Ficus Alii (hydrophobic soil), Ficus lyrata FLF (self-watering spike), Dracaena (drowning)

**Group C — Aroids**
- Terrarium (anaerobic decay), Pothos N'Joy + Neon (bare vines), Alocasia Frydek (nutrient depletion), Monstera Deliciosa + Adansonii (spike removal + prune), Philodendron Pink/Rojo (etiolated)

## The Cachepot System (Core Principle)

**(new) Never plant directly into decorative pots.** Use clear plastic nursery pots (10–20cm, heavy drainage slits) inside aesthetic cachepots (1–2 inches wider). Water in sink, drain fully, return. Eliminates root rot from standing water while maintaining visual aesthetic.

## Substrate Philosophy

**(new) Pre-mixed "houseplant soil" is too dense for ~80% of the collection.** Mix custom:
- Standard indoor potting soil
- Coarse perlite or pumice (drainage)
- Orchid bark (aroid/epiphyte aeration)

Ratio guideline: ~40% amendment for heavy-drinking species; higher (50–60%) for succulents and sansevierias.

## Lighting Zones (Amsterdam High-Latitude Context)

Winter light is weak/brief; summer diffused by clouds. Windows filter ~50% of UV. Rule: if you can't read a book without a lamp, a plant can't photosynthesize there.

| Zone | Location | Occupants |
|------|----------|-----------|
| Zone 1 (Prime) | Directly at S/W windows | Succulent, Ficus Tineke, FLF, Philodendron PPP, Monstera Adansonii |
| Zone 2 (Bright Ambient) | 1–2m back from S/W, or E windows | Monstera Deliciosa, Alocasia, Pothos, Ficus Alii, Staghorn |
| Zone 3 (Tolerant Shadows) | N windows / interior | ZZ Plant, Sansevieria Laurentii, Kirkii, Terrarium |

## Sprint Sequence (Half-Day Blocks)

**Sprint 1 — Morning (Wet/Messy):** Remove all watering spikes → soak station (Alii + Kirkii) → shower flush Monstera → root surgery (Laurentii + Tineke)

**Sprint 2 — Afternoon/Evening (Surgical/Dry):** Terrarium venting → succulent chop + callous → Pothos propagation → Philodendron stake → Ficus cutting cloche

**Sprint 3 — Day 3–4 (Nutrients/Epiphytes):** Staghorn repot in bark/moss → Alocasia N-heavy feed → plant calloused succulent cuttings → foliar cleaning

## Procurement List

- Clear plastic nursery pots multi-pack (10–20cm)
- Aesthetic cachepots (1–2 inches oversized)
- Bag perlite/pumice + orchid bark + standard indoor soil
- Microfiber cloths + liquid fertilizer (3-1-2 NPK ratio)
- Optional: full-spectrum LED grow bulb for darker corners
