# Hiphop Notes

---

- Sometimes the upper body actually drives the movement — work from there (especially on swings)
- Free the upper body — get it loose, not stiff as in ballet, to let the groove flow through
- Find the groove, not the movement shape — let the groove drive and lead the body parts
- Focus on musicality and beats — flow with it
- Soft knees; find the weight slightly more forward
- Range of movement — need to go down and forward more to feel the "down"
- Let the mid and lower back go — don't over-arch
- Don't tense up to generate movement — power is explosive and resolves after hitting the endpoint
