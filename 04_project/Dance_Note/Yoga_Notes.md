# Yoga Notes

---

## Poses & Corrections

- Dandasana: lower back long, lifted, and straight
- Supported halasana on chair and pillows — relaxes the back and neck
- Down dog: feet slightly wider, heels slightly off floor, find external rotation of the ankle
- Bridge: grab the mat and strap to open the top of the shoulder while keeping ribs neutral
- Back bend: outer foot presses down; tuck the tailbone
- Shoulderstand: left shoulder flexibility, relax left traps; do on thick mat and blanket; hold straps with palms up for support; keep back upright; shoulders contact the floor
- Side lift: tricep rotates outward; feel support from the underside; elbow eye pointing upward

---

## General Cues

- Lift the chest and belly in forward fold
- Stretch the armpit
- Shoulder turns inward to push away
- Feel the corset: wide back and front; lower abs in and down; upper abs up
- Standing: big toe presses down; outside of knee rotates outward while inner thigh rotates inward — helps backbend
- React to the quality and vibe of what's around you


## Shoulderstand — Hand Placement (Iyengar, 2026-06-27)

- Focus: extend both hands backward (behind the back) rather than squeezing shoulders inward toward center
- **Why**: The squeeze-inward approach causes excessive shoulder hyperextension. Reaching hands back creates a more open, supported backline.
- Result: More stable lift with less strain on shoulder joint (ref: [[2026-06-27]])
