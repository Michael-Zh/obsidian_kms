# Running Notes

---

## Technique

- Feet placed in front of the body; land with the whole foot
- Scratch the floor to drag back (hamstring-driven pull)
- Upper body loose
- Less arm swing; keep the shoulders straight

---

## Training Modes

Three types to rotate through:
1. **Easy/base run** — fixed distance, aerobic pace; builds endurance base
2. **Tempo run** — fixed time, sustained threshold effort; improves lactate clearance
3. **Sprint intervals** — short bursts at max effort; improves speed and power
