# EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

We've achieved **Level 1 success** in **transparency and coordination** between FBU and IBU teams and kicked start the **coverage** work stream in Q1.

However, we are **STILL** at the **starting stage** of the project, yet to see the effect of actual product changes and facing challenges of **project complexity** and **diminishing "bulk optimization" returns**.

We see that **insight analysis and proposal collection** while continuing the structural collaboration model can be a viable next step.
