## III. The Strategic Logic: "Clean Code" Move-In

You have identified that moving in together requires **Radical Transparency**.

- **The Logic:** Secrecy creates a "hidden data set." In a shared house, an ISFJ/Autistic partner will sense the "static" of your secret app usage. This leads to emotional friction that he cannot logically process.
    
- **The Solution:** An **Integrity Proposal**. Framing the open relationship not as an "exit ramp," but as an investment in total trust.
    

## IV. Tactical Framework: The ENM SLA (Service Level Agreement)

To protect his need for structure, the open relationship must be a "Module" with clear rules:

### 1. Scope of Engagement

- **Acts:** Specifically for penetration/dominance (experiences currently unavailable at home).
    
- **The "No-Emotion" Clause:** No dating, no overnights, no "emotional cuddling" with others.
    
- **Frequency Cap:** (e.g., 2x per month) to maintain the domestic routine.
    

### 2. Information Protocol (The TMI Guardrail)

- **The Question:** "What level of transparency makes you feel secure vs. what level makes you feel overwhelmed?"
    
- **Option A:** Full Data (When, where, who).
    
- **Option B:** "Need to Know" (Notification of absence, no details).
    

### 3. Re-Entry & Re-Affirmation

- **The Reset:** Mandatory shower/scent reset before interaction.
    
- **Parallel Play:** 15 minutes of quiet shared space (separate activities) to signal that he is the primary "Home Base."
    

## V. Cognitive Exercises

### Exercise A: The Empathetic Mirror

- **Context:** You find being "teased/touched" all the time stressful too.
    
- **Action:** Sit down and map out "Safe Touch Zones" and "Time Caps." Agree that 5 minutes of focused cuddling is better than 30 minutes of "low-quality" overstimulation.
    

### Exercise B: The "Safety Dial" (1–10)

- **Logic:** Allow him to turn the "volume" of your outside exploration up or down based on his mental capacity.
    
- **Level 1:** Monogamy (System maintenance).
    
- **Level 5:** Occasional Saunas/Hookups (Standard operation).

2. **The Disclosure Meeting:** Use the "I Statement" script. Frame it as: _"I want to move in with zero secrets so I can be 100% present with you."_

5. **Quarterly Review:** Every 3 months, audit the "Relational Equity." Is the system better, or is the "noise" too loud?
