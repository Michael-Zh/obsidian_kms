## Related Wiki Pages

**[[Fitness-Routine]]** — `02_wiki/PhysicalHealth/Fitness-Routine.md` — Structured fitness with recovery patterns and deloading
- **One variable per month** — Priority order: sleep/fasting → nutrition quality → training changes. Changing too many things at once kills the feedback loop.
- **Energy Crash Pattern** — Intensive exercise (gym or dance) reliably causes next-day fatigue. Two classes in succession = difficult energy for 2 days. Not a protocol failure — a known constraint.
- **Over-Planning meta-pattern** — "Perfect plan fallacy": tendency to plan exhaustively as anxiety management. The real challenge is adaptive execution, not a better plan. Same engine runs in work and relationships.

**[[Energy-Management]]** — `02_wiki/LifeManagement/Energy-Management.md` — Activation energy paradox and energy system design
- **Activation energy paradox** — Need energy to do things that create energy. Exercise creates energy but triggers next-day crash — plan around this, not against it.
- **Excited mind as superpower** — Don't silence it. Give it structured time to empty (Brain Dump) so body can rest. The goal is process, not suppression.
- **CO₂ & cognitive performance** — Ventilation matters for sustained work. Cheap, high-ROI intervention for long desk days before evening classes.

**[[Sleep-Optimization-Routine]]** — `02_wiki/LifeManagement/Sleep-Optimization-Routine.md` — Brain dump ritual and shutdown routine
- **Two-track protocol (new, Jun 2026):** Track A (home ≤10:30pm) uses 30-min phone timer + structured wind-down. Track B (home >10:30pm) accepts 1–2am naturally. Do NOT conflate them.
- **Brain Dump principle** — Once an idea is on paper, brain's job is done. Reduces overnight rehearsal loop that steals sleep.
- **Sleep failure is structural, not motivational** — Phone has no end condition; late eating from training schedule creates thermal/digestive delay. Fix the structure, not the willpower.
