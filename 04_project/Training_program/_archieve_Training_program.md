## 2. Nutrition, Fasting & Macro Architecture

**The Daily Target: ~2300 kcal (The Recomposition Sweet Spot)**

_Data proves this intake is simultaneously burning fat and building muscle. DO NOT drop calories further._

- **Protein (30%): ~175g** (Crucial: Protects your elite 43.8kg of muscle).
    
- **Carbs (40%): ~230g** (Glycogen for dance endurance and brain power).
    
- **Fats (30%): ~75g** (Hormonal health, joint lubrication).
    

### 2.1 The "Raw/Dry" Prime Directive (Accuracy Protocol)

- **Meat/Fish:** Weigh raw (loses ~25% water weight when cooked).
    
- **Rice/Pasta/Oats:** Weigh dry (gains 2.5x-3x water weight when cooked).
    

### 2.2 Linear Sequencing: The 2-Meal + 1-Snack Structure

**Meal 1 (12:00 PM) - The Structural Foundation (~900 kcal)**

- 250g Raw Chicken Thigh/Salmon + 100g Dry White Rice + Greens + 1/2 Avocado (or 1 tbsp Oil).
    

**Snack (15:30 PM) - The Liquid/Light Buffer (~500 kcal)**

- 200g 0% Greek Yogurt + 1 Scoop Whey + 1 Banana + 30g Dry Oats + 20g Nuts.
    

**Meal 2 (19:30 PM) - The Recovery Close (~900 kcal)**

- 250g Raw Lean Beef/White Fish + 400g Raw Potatoes/Sweet Potatoes (roasted) + 1 tbsp Butter/Oil + **5g Creatine.**
    

### 2.3 Option C: The Flexible Buffer Strategy

- **The Non-Negotiable:** Hit your Protein goal (170-180g) every day.
    
- **Intra-Workout:** Electrolyte Drink (Zero Sugar, 500mg+ Sodium) during Dance/Gym to stop the "sick" feeling.
