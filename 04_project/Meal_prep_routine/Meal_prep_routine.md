## 2. The Weekly Linear Action Sequence

### **Phase A: The Sunday Batch Cook**

1. **Prep Proteins:** Marinate 3kg chicken/salmon in **1.0L Steel** (use baking paper dividers).
    
2. **Cook Curry & Soup:** While protein marinate, cook Green Curry and Soup in large pots.
    
3. **Cooling (20 min):** Spread Fried Rice thin in **1.0L Steel** (lids off). Leave Curry/Soup in pots to stop steaming.
    
4. **The Portioning:**
    
    - **Curry:** Fill **4x 600ml Steel** (3cm gap!).
        
    - **Soup:** Fill **4x 600ml Steel** (3cm gap!).
        
    - **Fried Rice:** Fill **6x Monbento Plastic**.
        
5. **The "48-Hour" Rule:**
    
    - **Fridge:** 2 Rice, 1 Curry, 1 Soup.
        
    - **Freezer:** Everything else (Label immediately with tape).
        

### **Phase B: The Lasagna Protocol (When desired)**

1. **Assemble:** Layer directly into **600ml Steel** containers.
    
2. **Par-Bake:** Bake in the steel base (no lid) for 30 mins at 180°C.
    
3. **Cool & Freeze:** Once room temp, snap the lid on and freeze.
    
4. **Reheat:** Pop the "Lasagna Block" onto an office plate. Microwave 4 mins, then (if possible) Air Fry 3 mins.
    

## 3. The "Office Pop-and-Plate" Ritual

- **Sunday Night:** Move Monday's lunch from **Freezer to Fridge**.
    
- **The Commute:** Pack the "Slacked" Steel/Monbento into your bag. It stays cold but softens.
    
- **Lunchtime Execution:**
    
    1. Go to the office sink.
        
    2. Run **hot water** over the bottom of the Steel container for 10-15 seconds.
        
    3. Invert over an office ceramic bowl. **Press/Pop** the center.
        
    4. **Heat:** Microwave the bowl (easier to stir, more even heat).
        
    5. **Wash:** Rinse the steel container immediately—it’s now just a "shell" and takes 5 seconds to clean.
