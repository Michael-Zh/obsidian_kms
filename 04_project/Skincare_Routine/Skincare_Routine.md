### Storage & Stability Protocols

| Product | Storage | Reason | Duration |
|---|---|---|---|
| **C-Glow (Vitamin C)** | Refrigerate | Oxidizes rapidly | Permanent fridge |
| **Retinoid 2% Emulsion** | Fridge (open) or Cool Drawer (sealed) | Heat sensitive | Open: fridge; Backup: cool drawer |
| **Resveratrol + Ferulic** | Cool/Dark Drawer | Never refrigerate (crystallizes) | Room temp only |
| **Rose Hip Seed Oil** | Cool/Dark Drawer | Prevent solidification | Room temp only |
| **All other serums** | Cool/Dark Drawer | Standard stability | Room temp acceptable |
