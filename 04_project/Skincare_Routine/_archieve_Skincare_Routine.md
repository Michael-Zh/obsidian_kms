## Part 2: Product Storage Rules

_Chemical stability is critical. Improper storage leads to denatured proteins and oxidized antioxidants, resulting in wasted capital and potential skin irritation._

**❄️ MUST Refrigerate:**

1. **Geek & Gorgeous C-Glow:** Pure Vitamin C oxidizes rapidly. Keep in the fridge permanently.
    
2. **Granactive Retinoid 2% Emulsion:** Heat sensitive. Keep open bottles in the fridge. Unopened backups can stay in a cool, dark drawer.
    

**📦 Cool/Dark Drawer (Room Temp):**

1. **Resveratrol 3% + Ferulic Acid 3%:** _Never_ refrigerate. The lack of water means low temps cause crystallization.
    
2. **Rose Hip Seed Oil:** Keep at room temp to prevent solidification.
    
3. **All other serums:** Niacinamide, Peptides, Arbutin, etc.
    

_(Optional: Caffeine Solution can be refrigerated for an enhanced depuffing sensation)._
