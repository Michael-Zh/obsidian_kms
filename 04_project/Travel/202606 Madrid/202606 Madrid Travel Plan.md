## Part 4: Gay Saunas

**[Sauna Paraíso](https://maps.google.com/?q=Sauna+Paraíso,+Calle+del+Norte+15,+Madrid)** (Malasaña, 5 min from Chueca)
Visited Jul 4. Crowd quality and playroom atmosphere good. Facility condition poor — aged, under-maintained vs Berlin Boiler / Amsterdam standard. Not relaxing as a sauna experience. Worth it for the social scene; don't go for the facility.

Bear/kink crowd. Pool, Turkish bath, cinema, darkrooms. Busier during Pride. Try as Paraíso alternative.

24h, reliable. Good weekday wind-down option.

## Part 7: Transport

### Ticket recommendation

**Use contactless bank card (Visa/Mastercard).** Tap in/out at turnstiles — no purchase needed. ~€1.60/trip. ~7 metro trips total, ~€11. No leftover trips, no machine queues.

**Exception:** If your bank charges foreign transaction fees per tap, buy a **10-trip Metrobus card** at any metro machine instead (~€12.20). Covers the full trip.

**Tourist pass (Zone A, 5-day ~€26.80) — not worth it.** Need 18+ trips to break even; this itinerary uses ~7.

**Airport (T4):** Not covered by Zone A — taxi is simpler with luggage (~€30, ~25 min).
