# Sprint 1 — Execution Guide
**Morning of 2026-06-21 | Wet & Messy Triage**

Rule: Do all wet/messy work in the morning so plants have hours of warmth to drain before evening.

---

## Before You Start

- [ ] Buy potting soil at supermarket
- [ ] Lay down old towels or newspaper around sink/shower area
- [ ] Fill a bucket or use sink for soak station

---

## Step 1 — Spikes Already Pulled ✅
All watering spikes removed (done 2026-06-20).

---

## Step 2 — Soak Station (45 min, hands-off)
Start these first — they sit unattended while you do other things.

- [ ] **Ficus 'Alii'** — place in sink/bucket of lukewarm water, 45 min soak
- [ ] **Sansevieria Kirkii** — place alongside in lukewarm water, 45 min soak
- [ ] **Neon Pothos** — bottom-water only if pot feels feather-light

*While these soak: move to Step 3.*

---

## Step 3 — Flush Station (shower)
- [ ] **Monstera Deliciosa** — take to shower, run lukewarm water heavily through soil top-down for 2–3 minutes to flush stagnant minerals. Leave in shower to drain completely. Do not move until fully drained.

---

## Step 4 — Root Surgery: Sansevieria Laurentii
*Soil mix: 60% potting soil + 40% perlite*

- [ ] Unpot carefully — note any soft/dark roots
- [ ] Remove dead/rotting root tissue (dark, mushy = cut off)
- [ ] Let healthy roots air for 10 min
- [ ] Repot into **same original container** with amended soil mix (60/40)
- [ ] Do not water immediately — let settle 2–3 days

---

## Step 5 — Root Surgery: Ficus 'Tineke'
*Soil mix: 60% potting soil + 40% perlite*

- [ ] Unpot carefully
- [ ] Prune any dead lower leaves before starting
- [ ] Audit roots: remove rotting tissue, note extent of rot
- [ ] Repot into **same original container** with amended soil mix (60/40)
- [ ] Do not water for 5–7 days post-surgery

---

## Step 6 — Retrieve Soaked Plants
After 45 min:
- [ ] Remove Alii and Kirkii from soak, place on towel to drain
- [ ] Do not return to pots until fully drained (30+ min)
